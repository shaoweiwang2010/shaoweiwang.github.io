I am working as a postdoctoral researcher at SAIL at Queens University led by Prof. Ahmed E. Hassan. I obtained my Ph.D. from School of Information Systems, Singapore Management University under the supervision of Prof. David Lo and Prof. Lingxiao Jiang in 2015 and bachelor degree from Zhejiang University in 2010. I visited Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) in 2013.

My research focuses on: Machine Learning and Software Engineering. My goal is to help software developers retrieve and leverage software articles (e.g., source code, content on Q&A sites) effectively and efficiently. My research interests cover code search, software article analysis, program comprehension, software repository mining, secure software development, and malware detection.
